737-8 Max



The 737-8 Max is a plane that was grounded for a year, due to a system called the MCAS which would prevent the plane from stalling beacuse the engines were positioned too high on the wing. This caused 2 major crashes within a couple of months, which lead to the airplane being grounded for nearly two years. The aeroplane is flown by some major carriers like:
AirCanada,
Alaska Airlines,
Southwest,
American Airlines, and FlyDubai to name a few. The airplane has came back into service, and has a higher fuel efficiency, which is the reason they made the plane. They were competing with Airbus to make a very fuel efficient plane, Airbus making the A320neo(new engine option) and Boeing, the 737-Max lineup of planes.
